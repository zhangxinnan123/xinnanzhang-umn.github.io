I am a first-year Ph.D. student @ University of Minnesota, Twin Cities since Sep 2024, supervised by Prof. Mingyi Hong.

I obtained B.Eng in Artificial Intelligence and Electronic and Information Engineering in the University of Science and Technology of China. I was affiliated with the School of the Gifted Young, which originates from the first undegraduate program for special young talents in China.

My research interests include RLHF and Large Language Models (LLM). Previously I actively researched in the field of parameter estimation in signal processing.
